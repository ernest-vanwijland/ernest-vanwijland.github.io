Research Interests

My research hovers around the design of algorithms for optimization problems motivated by real-life applications or that arise in the machine learning context. I am currently working on clustering problems such as k-Means and k-Median, and on routing problems like Vehicle Routing and Multiple-Level Aggregation. I am especially interested in leveraging the geometric properties of the Euclidean space in the context of those problems. Previously, I have also worked on Fair Division and 2-WECSS.
